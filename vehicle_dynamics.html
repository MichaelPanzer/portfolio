Vehicle Dynamics

Intro:

As the suspension lead for the 25-26 RWR FSAE team, I was in charge of developing tire and vehicle dynamics models to guide our design. I structured the development timeline into two phases, tire modeling/selection and full vehicle dynamics. For our tire model I fit the MF6.1 tire model from Hans Pacejka's book Tire and Vehicle Dynamics to TTC data using in Python. I took this tire model and used it to create a 4-wheel steady state cornering model with parameterized suspension kinematics.
